How to Use
What is this app?

PAU Khad Guide helps you calculate the right amount of fertilizer for your crop based on your soil test report and target yield. It gives you crop-specific recommendations for Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P₂O₅), and Potassium (K₂O).

How to use — 3 simple steps
1Select your crop (Rice, Maize or Wheat) and, for Wheat, the rice residue management practice you follow.
2Enter your target yield in quintals per acre.
3Enter Soil N, Soil P and Soil K (kg/acre) from your PAU soil test report — or enter Organic Carbon % if Soil N is not available.
What you get
  • Recommended fertilizer doses in kg/acre (and kg/ha)
  • Ready-to-use product quantities — Urea, DAP or SSP, and MOP — so you know exactly how many bags to buy
  • Results in both kg/acre and kg/ha
Important tips
  • Always get your soil tested before applying fertilizers — it saves money and improves yield.
  • Use soil test values from an approved soil testing laboratory for best results.
  • If your soil nutrient level is high, the app may show zero dose for that nutrient — your soil already has enough; save the cost.
  • Target yield should be realistic for your field conditions — over-estimating leads to wasteful fertilizer use.
  • This app is calibrated for Punjab agro-climatic conditions and may not apply directly to other regions.
  • Recommendations assume normal crop management; abnormal weather, pest pressure or irrigation constraints may alter actual fertilizer response.
